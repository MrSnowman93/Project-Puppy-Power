About Puppy Power

Puppy Power is safe place for all players. We welcome you all to our sanctuary and all your roles and playstyles.We tend to keep our tone non-toxic, and we prefer to keep it that way.

Although we don’t pressure our players for high DPS outputs and certain tanking and healing set-ups, we offer to help you at your own will to advance in each of these roles, in your own pace of course.

Donations are not obligatory, we welcome them however if players wish to contribute to our community. In return we offer a free guild trader with 30 free slots to each member of our community.

Trials are often hosted on Saturdays, Mr-Borin starts collecting requests on Saturday morning, and tends to organise a group during the day. The Trial goes live at 5pm UK. If you booked your place for the Trial make sure to show up in time.

Sundays are booked for the progression groups, the main progression groups will be busy doing trials in their respective groups. However, all players are welcome to host trials every day of the week. Feel free to use our discord or guild chat to organise.

Players that are inactive over 3 weeks will be removed to make space for active players. If you are going on a vacation or have a planed absence that is longer than 3 weeks, please message admins so that they can take notes.
